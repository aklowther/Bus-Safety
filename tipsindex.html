Travel Tips:

You have already taken the first step in bus safety by downloading the EZ Rider app! This provides you with the ability to view stats on the carrier you may wish to travel with.

Safety:

1.) On longer trips, it is a good idea to have someone as a travel mate. Remember there're are safety in numbers.

2.) It is better to keep your opinion to your self and avoid conflict. Many people will say things you might not agree with.

3.) Be polite. Treat people with respect.

4.) Keep bags zipped and your belongings secure

5.) Be aware of emergency exits

Comfort:

1.) Don't sit next to the bathroom

2.) Don't sit at the back of the bus

3.) Bring a blanket and a travel pillow

4.) Pack earplugs or headphones

5.) Pack snacks
