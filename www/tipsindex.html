Travel Tips:

You have already taken the first step in bus safety by downloading the EZ Rider app! This provides you with the ability to view stats on the carrier you may wish to travel with.

Safety:

1.) On longer trips, it is a good idea to have someone as a travel mate. Remember there are safety in numbers.

2.) It is better to keep your opinion to your self and avoid conflict. Many people will say things you might not agree with.

3.) Be polite. Treat people with respect.

4.) Keep bags zipped and your belongings secure.

5.) Be aware of emergency exits.

Comfort:

1.) Do not sit near the restroom or at the back of the bus. The smell of the restroom or the drone of the engine may cause discomfort.

2.) Bring a blanket and a travel pillow. Bus companies keep their buses cold to reduce spread of germs.

3.) Use the restroom at rest stops. The restrooms on the bus are not always the most sanitary.

4.) Pack earplugs or headphones. There may be sounds you wish to block out to make your trip more enjoyable.

5.) Pack snacks. Depending on the length of your trip, the bus may or may not stop when you are hungry.
